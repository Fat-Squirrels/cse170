Assistance

At Brigham Young University-Idaho (BYU-Idaho), the concept of student belonging is integral to the campus community. The university strives to create an inclusive environment where every student feels valued and supported, regardless of their background.

This commitment to inclusivity is evident in the variety of resources and programs available to students, designed to foster a sense of belonging and community. From academic support services to diverse student organizations, BYU-Idaho ensures that each student has the opportunity to connect with others and engage fully in campus life.

The emphasis on shared values and mutual respect helps to cultivate a welcoming atmosphere where students can thrive both academically and personally. BYU-Idaho is dedicated to welcoming all students and ensuring they feel at home.

Get Help!

The university's Honor Code, while promoting high standards of personal conduct, is implemented with a spirit of love and acceptance. Faculty and staff are committed to providing a supportive educational experience, offering personalized attention and guidance to help students succeed.

Additionally, BYU-Idaho's focus on community service and faith-based initiatives encourages students to contribute positively to their surroundings, further enhancing their sense of belonging. This holistic approach ensures that every student at BYU-Idaho feels included and appreciated.

At BYU-Idaho, the combination of supportive resources, a welcoming atmosphere, and a commitment to personal and academic growth makes the university a place where all students can find their place and flourish.
