Academic

There are multiple ways to get help academically here at BYUI. Regardless of what your needs are there is always something that can be of assistance.

University Store

At the BYU-I Store you can find various items and essentials for your schooling. Visit the store here.

Tutors

At Brigham Young University-Idaho (BYU-I), tutors provide essential support in a range of subjects, offering personalized assistance to help students grasp complex concepts and excel academically. These services, available through one-on-one and group sessions, are designed to enhance understanding and build confidence in students.

Institute

The Institute of Religion at Brigham Young University-Idaho (BYUI) offers religious education and support to students, focusing on deepening their understanding of LDS Church teachings and applying those principles in their lives. It provides courses on scripture, church history, and doctrine, enhancing both spiritual growth and academic experience.

Staying in touch with a Student Life Advisor at BYU-Idaho provides essential guidance on academics, career goals, and personal development. Get in contact with one here.

Credit Hour to Study Rate

Credit Hour to Study Rate: This refers to the recommended amount of study time needed per week for each credit hour a student is enrolled in. For example, a standard guideline suggests that for each credit hour, students should allocate approximately 2-3 hours of study time outside of class per week to keep up with coursework effectively.

Societies, No Major Prerequisites

Societies, No Major Prerequisites: This typically indicates that certain student societies or clubs do not require specific major-related prerequisites for membership. Students from any academic background can join these societies, which often focus on various interests, networking, or extracurricular activities.

Grad planner

Grad Planner: A Grad Planner is a tool or resource used to help students plan their coursework and track their progress towards completing their degree requirements. It typically includes features for mapping out required classes, scheduling, and monitoring academic milestones to ensure timely graduation.

University store

University Store: The University Store is a campus facility where students can purchase textbooks, school supplies, university merchandise, and sometimes even food and dorm essentials. It serves as a convenient resource for meeting various academic and personal needs throughout the school year.
